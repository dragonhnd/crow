Crow

Crow looks into massive and streaming Twitter data, peck to find the trends and hot topics in each field, and visualize the statics to enable a bird's eye-view of Industries.

View the Project on GitHub supergrubby/crow

View the Trends of Latest Week

In the complicated and large-scale social network, there are always some leaders or authorities in every industry. What interests us are the topics they are talking about, which may indicate the current hot issues or predict future trends. This analysis can inspire researchers and help companies to make plans on development and marketing. Here we can see the trends of latest week.

Hot Topics

Since large-scale data in social network comes rapidly and continuously, it is hard to capture and analyze. There is a need to capture it in time and present it as readable tables or charts. Our system provides solutions to explore popular topics in certain industry and show results user-friendly.

Authorities and Leaders

Twitter, as one of the most popular social network service, owns 316 million monthly active users and 500 million tweets sent by day (stated in official website). It is chosen as representative platform to study. Picking words or phrases from user’s tweets, number of mentioning can be ranked. Furthermore, analyzing industry leaders’ following and follower relationship helps to list out other powerful people. Analysis can be conducted hierarchically. We are tracking and analyzing these users' Twitter.
